# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in the project.

We are currently looking for contributors in:

- AI / NLP;
- RAG;
- automated fact-checking;
- backend development;
- decentralized systems;
- AI safety;
- cryptoeconomics.

## How to contribute

1. Open an issue.
2. Comment on an existing issue.
3. Propose a small improvement.
4. Share relevant research.
5. Build a prototype module.

## Communication
